The Best Movies According to Fadila

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Notebook

One of my all time favourite romance movies that depicts the toxicity and love partners have for one another. It describes how partners can love yet still not be good for each other, but still be there for each other for as long as time is still in their favour.

Spirited Away

My favourite anime movie which show all the plots and twists one movie has.

The Perks of Being a Wallflower

My favourite comfort movie that isn't really extraordinary, but is still in my top 3 whenever I want to experience what high school feels like again.
